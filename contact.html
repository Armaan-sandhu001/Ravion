Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common queries

How long does delivery take?

Standard delivery takes 5-7 business days. Premium and VIP members get faster delivery in 3-5 business days. All orders include tracking.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day hassle-free return policy. Items must be unworn with tags attached. Refunds are processed within 5-7 days of receiving the return.

Do you offer bulk orders?

Yes! We offer special pricing for bulk and corporate orders. Contact us with your requirements using the "Bulk / Corporate Order" subject above.

How do I track my order?

Once shipped, you'll receive a tracking link via email and SMS. You can also check order status in your profile under "Recent Orders".

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Cash on Delivery, Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, Mastercard, Rupay), UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm), and Net Banking.

How do I become a VIP member?

Visit our Membership page to explore Basic, Premium, and VIP plans. VIP members get exclusive drops, free shipping, and member-only prices.
